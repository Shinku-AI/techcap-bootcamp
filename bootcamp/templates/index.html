Welcome

Designed with beginners in mind, this bootcamp is the perfect launchpad for anyone looking to dive into the world of coding. Whether you're aspiring to switch careers, enhance your current job profile, or simply curious about programming, our program caters to all levels from absolute beginners to intermediate coders. Over the course of two months, during convenient weekend sessions, you will transition from learning the basics of Python to mastering skills that will empower you to apply Python effectively in any field you choose.

Our bootcamp stands out because it's led by fellow students who have shown exceptional aptitude in coding, ensuring you learn from those who have recently navigated the same learning path. This unique peer-led format fosters an engaging and supportive learning environment, enabling you to grasp complex concepts more comfortably and confidently. As a participant, you'll not only gain a solid foundation in Python but also learn how to think like a programmer, opening up a world of possibilities in tech. While we haven't set a registration deadline yet, spots are filling up fast. Join us for this in-person, transformative journey with the TechCap Initiative and take your first step towards becoming a proficient Python programmer.
